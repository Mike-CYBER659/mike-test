ABOUT THE COMPANY

Apple Inc. is a globally renowned technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple began as a personal computer pioneer and has since evolved into one of the most valuable and influential companies in the world. Its early success was marked by the Apple II and later the Macintosh, which introduced a graphical user interface and revolutionized the way people interacted with computers. Over the years, Apple expanded its product line to include a range of consumer electronics, software, and digital services. The company is best known for its iconic devices such as the iPhone, iPad, MacBook, and Apple Watch. Each of these products has contributed to shaping modern technology and design standards. Apple's iOS operating system, along with its App Store, has created a thriving ecosystem that supports millions of developers and users worldwide.
