OUR CEO

Tim Cook is the CEO of Apple and serves on its board of directors. Before being named CEO in August 2011, Tim was Apple’s chief operating officer and was responsible for all of the company’s worldwide sales and operations, including end-to-end management of Apple’s supply chain, sales activities, and service and support in all markets and countries. He also headed Apple’s Macintosh division and played a key role in the continued development of strategic reseller and supplier relationships, ensuring flexibility in response to an increasingly demanding marketplace. Prior to joining Apple, Tim was vice president of Corporate Materials for Compaq and was responsible for procuring and managing all of Compaq’s product inventory.

about our company

Apple Inc. is a globally renowned technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple began as a personal computer pioneer and has since evolved into one of the most valuable and influential companies in the world. Its early success was marked by the Apple II and later the Macintosh, which introduced a graphical user interface and revolutionized the way people interacted with computers. Over the years, Apple expanded its product line to include a range of consumer electronics, software, and digital services. The company is best known for its iconic devices such as the iPhone, iPad, MacBook, and Apple Watch. Each of these products has contributed to shaping modern technology and design standards. Apple's iOS operating system, along with its App Store, has created a thriving ecosystem that supports millions of developers and users worldwide. Innovation and design are at the core of Apple’s brand identity. The company places a strong emphasis on aesthetics, user experience, and seamless integration across its devices. This approach has helped Apple cultivate a loyal customer base and maintain a premium image in the market. Regular product launches and updates, often announced at high-profile events, keep consumers and tech enthusiasts eagerly anticipating the next big innovation. In addition to hardware, Apple has increasingly focused on services, including Apple Music, iCloud, Apple Pay, and Apple TV+. These services diversify its revenue streams and reinforce the company's ecosystem. Environmental sustainability, data privacy, and ethical supply chain management have also become key priorities. As a leader in the tech industry, Apple continues to influence consumer trends, drive innovation, and set benchmarks for competitors.
